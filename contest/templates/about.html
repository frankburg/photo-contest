WE SHARE MEMORY FOR THE CAUSE
National Photo Contest is an innovative contest that aims and strives to reinvent the spark in people’s life and their use of modern technology by giving them an opportunity to use photos to express themselves, their daily activity and emotions. Photos of a specific subject are collected and rated.  Photos considered are photos taken with mobile devices (Android, iPhone, iPad devices and HD Cameras as well). This year edition which is the maiden edition is tagged “Actions at Workplace” targeted towards youths and young adults. This focuses on photos that captures memorable moments or activities at workplace, market, and business centers. The National Photo Contest nurtures an aspirational community that supports individuals who wish to share important stories and influence the way people think through images.
